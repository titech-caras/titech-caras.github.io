Welcome! CARAS is a research group at Science Tokyo (formerly Tokyo Tech) in the Department of Information and Communications Engineering. Our objective is to improve the security, energy-efficiency and dependability of next generation computing devices and systems. We conduct research in all aspects of computer systems, ranging from computer architecture, operating systems to compilers.

**We are  looking for new students to join our group! Please check the message on [Hiroshi's website](https://hiroshi-sasaki.github.io/index-en.html) and feel free to [contact him](mailto:sasaki@ict.eng.isct.ac.jp) with your CV if you are interested in doing cutting-edge research in computer architecture, computer security and computer systems at Science Tokyo!**
